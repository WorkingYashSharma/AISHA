AISHA Responses : tell me about artificial intelligence 
 *************************



Artificial Intelligence (AI) is a broad term used to describe the ability of computers and machines to think and learn. It is the science and engineering of making intelligent machines, especially computer programs. AI can be used to solve complex problems, such as recognizing objects in an image, understanding natural language, and controlling robots. AI can also be used to automate tasks that were previously done by humans. AI has been used in a variety of applications, such as computer vision, natural language processing, robotics, and machine learning. AI is also being used in healthcare, finance, and other industries. AI is becoming increasingly important in our lives and is changing the way we interact with technology.
